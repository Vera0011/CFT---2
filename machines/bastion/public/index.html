What payment methods do you accept?

                        
⟁ We accept Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH) and Monero (XMR). We strongly
                            recommend XMR for maximum anonymity. No fiat, no credit cards, no PayPal — ever.

                    

                    

                        
How are digital products delivered?

                        
⟁ After payment confirmation (typically 1–3 blockchain confirmations),
                            download links are generated and accessible from your account dashboard. Links expire after
                            72 hours and are single-use.

                    

                    

                        
Can I get a refund?

                        
⟁ Due to the digital nature of our products, refunds are not issued once a

⟁ Our tools and services are designed for legitimate security research and
                            penetration testing with explicit written authorization. Misuse is the sole responsibility
                            of the purchaser. Know your local laws.

                    

                    

                        
How do I report a vulnerability in your platform?
                        

                        
⟁ Encrypt your report with our PGP key and send it to ops@hacked.sa with
                            subject line [VDP]. We run a private responsible disclosure program. Researchers who report
                            critical vulnerabilities may receive compensation in XMR.
